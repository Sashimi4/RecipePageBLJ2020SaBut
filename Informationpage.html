Introduction


Hello and Welcome to my Bakery website. I created this website for a small project. The idea of it is to improve my HTML and CSS skills. The purpose of the website itself is to post weekly a new recipe i learnt and share it with others. This week as a small hint it's a ready for winter and has a peculiar design and texture, but is a classic and beginner-friendly. The concept art of this website is located over in my personal figma account. I tried to go for a modern look. 2020 website trends are definitly sleek, modern, simplistic yet complex and monochromatic. Since it's christmas i chose the color combination of red and white. They are very distinguishable colors which have a mild conflict, that in return make the user/viewer more interested. I built in some simple javascript thanks to youtube tutorials. I am really happy with how the navbar came out. The simple ease in and color flip brings dynamic movement into a static website and give it life. A big Thanks goes also to the website Pexel. Platform with free-use photos. My website would be nothing without those great photo galleries. At the bottom of the page I will link some Photographers. I didn;t use all photos from every Photographer but to the ones that at the end did'nt get used. I will also be mentioning them, since they played a big role in inspiration and the humble beginnings of this site, which you are reading. Interested in more of my work? Contact me. Otherwise..... enjoy this weeks bakery
